# Shopkeeper Showdown — Keywords

Keywords are universal game terms that appear on cards and abilities throughout the game. Each is defined once here.

---

## Appraise X

Look at the top 4 resources of the resource deck. Keep X of them. Return the rest in any order on top of the deck.

Used by: Workshop (Appraise 4), Spirited Summoner (Appraise 3), Monk Momentum spend, Sorcerer abilities.

---

## Auction

Choose 1 resource from your windows or hoard. Roll d6. Sell the chosen resource to the discard pile for the rolled coin value. If the resource has a Reputation icon, gain the matching Rep token.

Used by: Tavern (Auction 1), Counterfeit "Orcish Bitter" return effect.

---

## Break X

Mark X of another player's windows with a Broken token. A Broken window cannot be sold from until repaired. The window itself remains in place; only its function is disabled.

Used by: Thieves' Guild (Steal 1 / Break 1), Barbarian (Reckless Swing), Sorcerer (Hot Streak failed-guess), Monk Momentum spend, Shady Saboteur Professional, Counterfeit "Sparkling Water" return effect.

---

## Distribute

Take a resource from the Flea Market and place it onto a Visitor's order to reduce their remaining quota by 1. No coins are earned, but Visitor completion bonuses still trigger as normal. If no Visitor on the board will accept that resource type, discard the resource instead.

Used by: Charismatic Clerk, Polite Promoter Professionals.

---

## Draw X

Take X cards from the top of the resource deck into your hand or directly into windows/hoard at your discretion.

Used by: Many abilities and return effects.

---

## Fence

Sell 1 Stolen resource from your Hoard to the discard pile. The resource type must be DIFFERENT from the resource currently on top of the Flea Market. Gain the coin value of the resource. Ignore any Reputation icon. The Stolen marker is removed on sale.

Used by: Thieves' Guild action only.

---

## Forage X

Look at the top 4 resources of the discard pile. Keep X of them. Return the rest in any order on top of the discard pile.

Used by: Wilderness (Forage 2), Shaman (Patience of Stone alternative).

---

## Gather

Roll your d6. Draw that many resources from the top of the resource deck.

Used by: Wilderness (main action), Ranger passive (half roll, round down), Marvellous Mascot Professional.

---

## Launder X

Draw X resources from the top of the resource deck. Mark them all as Stolen.

Used by: Thieves' Guild (Launder 2), Resourceful Recruiter Professional, Sorcerer (Reality Ripple), Counterfeit "Fake Ring" return effect.

---

## Refresh

Ready 1 spent Active token by flipping it face-up. Tavern's "Refresh Actives" readies ALL spent Active tokens at once. Other Refresh effects refresh exactly 1 unless specified.

Used by: Tavern (Refresh Actives), Pitch Camp, Counterfeit "Prop Sword" return effect, Clash winner, various class abilities.

Note: Monk does not have Active tokens. Any effect that would grant Refresh to the Monk grants 1 Momentum instead.

---

## Repair X

Remove a Broken token from X of your own windows. The window returns to normal function.

Used by: Alluring Alchemist (Repair 1), Report the Crime at Barracks (Repair ALL option), Shaman Elemental Die result 3 (Repair 2), Renown card effects.

Note: Repair can also be triggered by paying 3 coins at any location (a universal rule, not a keyword).

---

## Steal X

Take X resources at random from a chosen player's Hoard. Mark them as Stolen. The Night Watcher badge holder cannot be targeted.

Used by: Thieves' Guild (Steal 1), Counterfeit "Fake Fur Harness" return effect, Reactive Steal (off-turn ability), various class abilities.

---

## Trade X

Swap X of your resources (from windows or hoard) with the same number of resources from the Flea Market. Your resources go to where the Flea Market resources came from, and vice versa.

Used by: Tavern (Trade 2), Polite Promoter, Alluring Alchemist, Counterfeit "Back Door Key" return effect, Shaman Elemental Die result 2 (Trade 5), Monk Momentum spend (Trade 2).

---

## Other terms

### Active token
Each player has 2 Active tokens. Spent to use Active class abilities or Reactive Steal. Refreshed at the Tavern or via Refresh effects. Monk uses Momentum instead.

### Hoard
Off-board resource storage, maximum 8 cards. Resources here cannot be sold directly but can be crafted with, traded, fenced, or used for class abilities. Stolen resources go here face-up.

### Window
One of 5 shop display slots per player. Resources placed here are available for selling during the Sell Phase. A window can be Shuttered (closed, immune to Steal/Break for one turn) or Broken (cannot be sold from until repaired).

### Stolen marker
A marker placed on a resource that was Stolen or Laundered. The resource sells normally to Visitors and at Auction (Stolen marker is removed on sale). At the Thieves' Guild, Stolen resources can be Fenced. At the Barracks, Report the Crime can force the discard of a Stolen resource. Counterfeits are inherently Stolen but cannot be discarded by Report the Crime.

### Reactive Steal (off-turn)
When stolen from, you may spend 1 Active token to immediately steal 1 resource at random from the attacker's Hoard. No coins are gained, and no Stolen marker is applied.

### Clash
When you end your turn on a space shared with other players, a Clash may trigger. All players there roll d6 (plus modifiers). Highest wins: take 1 resource from each other player's Hoard and refresh 1 Active. Ties result in no effect.

### Night Watcher
A single badge held by one player at a time (3+ player games only). The holder cannot be the NEXT target of Steal or Break. As soon as anyone else is targeted by Steal or Break, the badge moves to them. On multi-target effects, the instigator chooses where the badge ends up. The Hire Bodyguard action at the Barracks lets a player take the badge for themselves for 2 coins.
